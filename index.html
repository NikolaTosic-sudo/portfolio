What it means to be a
Developer

People think it's all about building applications, web-sites etc... I have to disagree. It's more about connecting with people. Everything built is meant to be interacted by people, it's the same with web-sites and applications.

Never stop
Learning

This is something I love about this career. There is always something new to learn. Old saying goes - "Jack of all trades, master of none", but this one is actually incorrect. Real goes like this - "Jack of all trades, master of one". That is exactly how it should be, practice everything, know a lot, but be a master in one.

A lot of people who stop at being master of one "trade". Not me, I'm driven to always improve, to always learn new things, to bring new things to table, whatever it might be. For now I'm only "Jack of a lot of trades", but I'm not stopping and I never will.

In the end. Of course, best way to learn is to work with different people, even complete beginner will bring different view about something that can change the course of the project/language or even the whole concept of developing. I don't even have to mention experienced developers, who can teach young ones the best ways of learning, coding, even behaving in workplace, with others and with the code.
